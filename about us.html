OUR MARKETING::
Until 2019 we used to sold our product at Anakapalli Jaggery Market
which is the 2nd largest jaggery market in INDIA.
From 2019 we had started our Local Marketing and as well as bulk orders for Functions,shopkeepers,sweet shops...

OUR PROCESS::
We used to collect the sugar cane juice from the fresh
sugar cane and we used to boil it for three and half to
four hours by removing the wastage from it. and dry into
the required shapes and weights.
